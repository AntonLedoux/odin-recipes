Fried eggs with bacon, cheese, and tomatoes

A traditional breakfast

Easiest way to impress someone with your cooking skills. Also the easiest breakfast possible.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Bacon
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Tomatoes

Cooking steps

  1. Slice the tomatoes.
  2. Shred the mozarella cheese.
  3. Fry the eggs.
  4. Add bacon to the pan.
  5. Add tomatoes and cheese to the pan when eggs are almost done.
